Section 01

Overview

TumblerPigeon ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile application designed to help pigeon enthusiasts manage their flock — including individual pigeon records, breeding pairs, egg clutches, training sessions, and pedigree charts.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our app, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using TumblerPigeon, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

📌 We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

📦
Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the app's core features:

Category Data Collected Purpose
Account Info Email address, hashed password Authentication and account security
Pigeon Records Ring number, breed name, gender, color, hatch date, status, health notes Core flock management feature
Photos Images of pigeons uploaded by you Visual identification of birds in your flock
Breeding Data Pair records, egg clutch data, egg statuses Breeding and reproduction tracking
Training Logs Session type, start/end time, duration, notes Training session history
Pedigree Data Parent-child relationships between pigeon records Lineage and genealogy charts

We do not collect location data, contacts, call logs, SMS, microphone audio, browsing history, or any sensitive personal information beyond what is listed above.

Section 03

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to operate the app's features:

  • Create and manage your personal flock records
  • Authenticate you securely and protect your account
  • Store and display pigeon photos you upload
  • Build breeding pair and pedigree relationships
  • Record and display training sessions
  • Sync your data across reinstalls of the app

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the app's core functionality.

Section 05

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on Supabase-managed servers with Row-Level Security (RLS) enforced at the database level — this means your data is only accessible to your own account.

  • Passwords are never stored in plain text — Supabase handles secure hashed authentication
  • All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Pigeon photos are stored in a private-access Supabase Storage bucket
  • Database rows are protected by Row-Level Security policies scoped to your user ID

⚠️ While we apply industry-standard security practices, no digital system can guarantee 100% security. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

🗓️
Section 06

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated records — pigeon profiles, photos, breeding data, training logs, and pedigree information — will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

To request account deletion, contact us at the email address in Section 10.

Section 07

Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can view all data you have entered directly within the app
  • Correction: You can edit any pigeon record, pair, or training log at any time within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete individual records within the app, or request full account deletion by contacting us
  • Data portability: You may request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Withdraw consent: You may stop using the app and request deletion of all your data at any time

If you are located in the European Union (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you also have rights under the GDPR / UK GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
